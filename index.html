About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Ben and I am a student at the Penn LPS Coding Bootcamp. I am new to coding, but am extremely excited to push myself throughout the course. I grew up in Abington, PA and currently live in the Fairmount area of Philadelphia. I attended the University of Pennsylvania and graduated in 2015 as a Cognitive Science Major. Before enrolling in this course I spent my time in a few different jobs including with the 76ers organization, Northwestern Mutual and as a professional caddie on the Web.com Golf Tour. I am pumped for all of the opportunities and new career paths that this course will open up for me!

Although I am spending a lot of my free time focusing on this course, I am also very passionate about the game of golf - I still play a number of tournaments, when my schedule allows. While golf is the one sport I spend my time actually playing, I also spend a lot of time watching most sports. I have always been, and remain, a huge Philadelphia sports fan. Go Sixers! Another thing I am passionate about is pets - there was a brief time I thought about going to veterinary school. I spent a summer interning for the Penn Vet Working Dog Center -- in this very Pennovation Facility!!

Well that's enough about me - now I need to focus on the material so I can improve with all of this.
